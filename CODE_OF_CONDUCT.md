# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Being respectful and constructive in discussions
- Providing and accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the project and community

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers will remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations will result in a ban from the project.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it by opening a private issue or contacting the maintainer directly.
